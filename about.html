More Than Just Games

In a world that is increasingly digital, we found ourselves missing the simple joy of sitting across a table from friends and family. Connect More Cafe was born in Franklin, TN, out of a desire to create a "third space"—a place that isn't work and isn't home, where you can truly engage with the people around you.

We believe that board games are more than just cardboard and plastic; they are tools for connection, laughter, and building community. Whether you’re a lifelong strategy gamer or someone who haven't picked up a dice since childhood, our mission is to provide an environment where everyone feels welcome to "Stay & Play."

Our Mission

"In an increasingly disconnected world, Connect More Game Cafe offers a vital solution: a vibrant community hub where individuals, friends, and families can forge meaningful connections through the joy of tabletop gaming. We provide a curated selection of games, expert guidance, and a welcoming atmosphere that encourages face-to-face interaction, laughter, and the creation of lasting memories."
